We are seeking an experienced Smart Transport Systems Developer to join our UrbanPulse Dynamics team. You will be responsible for designing and developing digital platforms that support smart transport solutions for councils and urban communities across Australia.

Salary and Reporting Line

Salary: $85,000 - $100,000 per annum (based on experience)

Reports to: Senior Smart City Solutions Manager

Key Responsibilities

  1. Design and develop web-based platforms for smart transport monitoring
  2. Integrate real-time transport data feeds into digital dashboards
  3. Collaborate with council partners to identify transport system needs
  4. Conduct testing and performance reviews of transport platforms

Essential Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or Engineering
  • Minimum 2 years experience in web platform development
  • Strong proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Experience working with real-time data systems

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience working with local councils or government partners

  • Familiarity with GIS or mapping technologies
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum project management
